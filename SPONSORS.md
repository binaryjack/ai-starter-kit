# Sponsors

AI Agencee is free and open-source software. Sponsorship keeps the project maintained, documented, and growing.

---

## Why Sponsor?

- The CLI, engine, MCP bridge, and all packages are **free forever** under the MIT licence
- Sponsorship directly funds maintainer time for bug fixes, new features, documentation, and community support
- Enterprise organisations with compliance requirements benefit most from the audit logging, RBAC, PII scrubbing, and multi-tenant isolation built into the engine — sponsoring signals that you value this work being sustainable

## Legal & Tax Notes (Switzerland)

All sponsorship funds are received by **AI Agencee**, based in Switzerland.

- Sponsorship payments are **taxable income** for the recipient under Swiss federal and cantonal tax law (DBG Art. 16; cantonal equivalents)
- **VAT (MWST)**: sponsorship is currently below the CHF 100,000 annual threshold for mandatory Swiss VAT registration. No VAT is added to GitHub Sponsors payments at this time. This will be updated if/when VAT registration applies.
- **Sponsors in the EU**: if your organisation is VAT-registered and you receive a counter-performance (logo, support), this may be a B2B service subject to reverse-charge VAT in your jurisdiction. Consult your tax adviser.
- **Receipts**: GitHub Sponsors provides receipts via Stripe. If you require a Swiss-format invoice (e.g. with CHF amount, reference to AHV/VAT number), contact the maintainer.
- This is **not** a donations structure via a foundation or association (Verein/Stiftung). There is no charitable deductibility under Swiss law.
